Our Donate Plan

We have some official and non-official donation plan for socity. We always prefer child education, child nutrition, social security and respont on regional crisis.

Food for a family

According to the FAO, 40 million (27 per cent) Bangladeshis are already undernourished. That means not having access to adequate amounts of safe, nutritious food to sustain a healthy and productive life.

Shelter for a family

Bangladesh, which is one of the poorest countries in the world, is faced with a critical housing crisis of long standing. It has been estimated that as many as three-quarters of the urban population are unable to contribute anything toward their housing due to their low incomes.

Education for Child

In Bangladesh, although primary education is free and the government provides the textbooks, more than 4.3 million children aged 6-15 years are not in school and around 42 million people – about 26 percent of the population – are still illiterate.

About Us

We started our journey in 2018 with a mission to address the health care needs and human rights issues of sexual minority populations for achieving a vision of a Bangladesh where every person, irrespective of their gender and sexuality, is able to lead a quality life with dignity, human rights and social justice.
